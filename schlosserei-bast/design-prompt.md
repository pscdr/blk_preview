## Design-Prompt: Schlosserei Bast
**Style: Neo-Geo**

Conceive a single-page website for Schlosserei Bast, a second-generation master metalworking shop rooted in the Westerwald region of Germany, rendered through the lens of Neo-Geo design — where refined geometric patterns and mathematical beauty become the visual language for precision craftsmanship. The page should evoke a sense of calculated confidence and quiet authority as visitors arrive, immediately feeling the intersection of geometric order and artisanal mastery. The visual hierarchy flows through interlocking angular forms and clean structural grids that mirror the very metal constructions Bast creates — staircases, railings, balconies, gates, and custom garage doors. Ground the palette in deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) as the voice of forged steel, punctuated by a commanding red (#c8102e) that pulses through call-to-action elements like heated metal in the forge, while a warm burnished gold (#d4a843) threads through accent details as a nod to the company's heritage and DIN EN 1090 certified quality. Introduce colorful geometric overlays and patterned section dividers that transform the scroll into a journey through an architect's blueprint — precise, intentional, and inherently beautiful. Display the Schlosserei Bast logo prominently as the anchor of this geometric system, loading from its original source.

The typographic system should feel engineered yet approachable — select a geometric sans-serif from Google Fonts (such as Inter or Space Grotesk) that mirrors the mathematical precision of Neo-Geo while remaining warm enough for a family-run Meisterbetrieb. Headings should land with the weight of structural steel, bold and unapologetic, while body text breathes with generous spacing that feels like walking through a well-designed workshop where every tool has its place. The single-page journey should emotionally progress from awe (the hero section with workshop imagery showcasing their craftsmanship) through trust (DIN EN 1090 certification, second-generation legacy, team of trained specialists) into inspiration (the nine service categories displayed as a geometric grid of real project photographs) and finally to warm invitation (contact details, workshop hours, the promise of individual consultation). No animations — the design speaks through static precision. Each section transition should feel like turning a precisely machined part, revealing a new facet of the same unified form.

Draw inspiration from the emotional quality of Bauhaus-era industrial design exhibitions where function and beauty were inseparable, the satisfying geometry of well-drafted engineering blueprints, and the dignified atmosphere of a master craftsman's workshop where decades of tradition are visible in the orderly arrangement of specialized tools. Think of the confidence that radiates from precision-welded joints, the mathematical poetry of a perfectly constructed spiral staircase, the way Corten steel ages into geometric patterns of warm rust against clean architectural lines. These abstract references should infuse the final design with a feeling of engineered elegance — not cold or clinical, but warm in the way that handcrafted metal objects carry the warmth of their maker's expertise. The page should feel like stepping into a space where geometry serves humanity, where every angle has been considered, and where the tradition of two generations of master metalworkers is expressed through the universal language of refined mathematical form.
