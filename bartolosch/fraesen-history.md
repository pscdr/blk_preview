# Bartolosch Maschinen GmbH

40 years of German mechanical engineering expertise are the guarantee for Bartolosch's international success.

## We stand for

We supply the demanding sheet metal processing market worldwide. A strong management, highly motivated employees, constant willingness to innovate and the high level of training of our employees guarantee our customers competence and creativity in all areas of profile technology.

### Our origin

- Bartolosch Maschinen GmbH was taken over by the Bartolosch family in 1978.
- The company is located in the beautiful Westerwald region between Cologne and Frankfurt.
- A company of the P. Meth Group
- Production, assembly and sales – all from one company
- Over 240 employees in the group

## Our Story

Bartolosch Maschinen GmbH is one of the leading manufacturers of machines and equipment for standing seam technology.

In 1979 Bartolosch GmbH, owner Mr. Ing. Friedhelm Bartolosch, took over the rights of the company Bartolosch, Heilbronn and founded the company „Bartolosch-Maschinen GmbH" – machines for folding technology. The new location Friedewald is situated in the Westerwald.

The big breakthrough came with the development of the first width-adjustable profiling machine in 1981. Further developments: round sheet forming machine (1985), EHA notching unit (1989), QUADRO profiling machine (1997).

The Bartolosch company is managed by Patric Meeth and Christoph Gerigk. Together with Bartolosch GmbH, the company employs around 200 people on a production area of 10,000 square meters.
