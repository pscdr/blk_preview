## Design-Prompt: STEREON Glassfilters
**Style: Neo-Geo (Refined Geometric Patterns, Mathematical Beauty)**

Embrace the Neo-Geo design style to craft a single-page website for STEREON Glassfilters GmbH — a precision manufacturer of ROBU® sintered glass filter elements for chemical, pharmaceutical, and laboratory applications. The page should evoke a feeling of scientific exactness married with quiet elegance: visitors arriving should sense the mathematical certainty and crystalline clarity that defines both borosilicate glass and the Neo-Geo aesthetic. The visual hierarchy should unfold through precise geometric compositions — hexagonal grids, circular motifs echoing glass pore structures, and intersecting angular forms — rendered in STEREON's refined palette of steel-grey (#666977) as the grounding primary tone, punctuated by confident red (#FF0000) accents that draw the eye to critical interaction points, all resting on a clean light background (#F4F4F4) with deep charcoal (#2C2C2C) typography set in Source Sans Pro. Incorporate colorful geometric elements — subtle red-tinted shapes, graduated grey patterns, and translucent overlapping forms — that mirror the precision filtration products STEREON creates, making the geometry itself feel purposeful rather than decorative.

The design philosophy should treat every pixel as an engineer treats every micron — nothing is arbitrary, everything serves function, yet the result transcends utility into visual sophistication. Typography in Source Sans Pro should feel authoritative and technically precise, with generous tracking on headings that gives them a laboratory-specification quality, while body text remains warm and approachable enough for international scientific professionals seeking filtration solutions. Interactions and micro-animations should feel crystalline and exact — geometric shapes that rotate with mechanical precision, grid lines that draw themselves with the confidence of a technical blueprint, elements that snap into position with satisfying mathematical certainty. The single-page journey should progress from an arresting geometric hero composition featuring STEREON's laboratory imagery, through clean content blocks presenting their ISO-standard filtration products, their heritage transitioning from ROBU to STEREON within the STEREON MATERIALS group, their product finder and ordering capabilities, down to a final call-to-action that feels like the logical conclusion of a perfectly structured proof — inevitable and compelling.

Draw inspiration from the feeling of walking through a precision optics laboratory where every surface is immaculate and every instrument is perfectly calibrated — that sense of controlled excellence. Channel the aesthetic spirit of mid-century scientific illustration where complex phenomena were rendered with geometric clarity and understated beauty. Think of the quiet authority of Bauhaus-adjacent industrial design, the satisfying order of crystallographic diagrams, the visual rhythm of circuit boards viewed under magnification — patterns that are simultaneously functional and mesmerizing. Reference the emotional quality of premium scientific instruments: the weight and precision of a brass microscope, the clarity of optically perfect glass, the confidence that comes from certified standards (ISO 4793, ISO 3585, ASTM E 128). These abstract references should infuse the final design with an atmosphere where STEREON's deep expertise in sintered glass technology is felt not through words alone, but through a visual language that speaks fluency in precision, quality, and scientific rigor — a single page that itself feels like a perfectly engineered product.
