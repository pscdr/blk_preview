## Design-Prompt: Unternehmensberatung Braun
**Style: Gradient Modern**

Adopting a Gradient Modern aesthetic, conceive a single-page landing page for Unternehmensberatung Braun GmbH — an owner-managed management consultancy specializing in public transport (ÖPNV) advisory, SME consulting, controlling, and business valuation, founded by Markus Braun in the Westerwald region of Germany. The core emotional quality should be one of quiet authority and forward momentum: as visitors arrive, they encounter a deep navy (#00263E) that seamlessly transitions through rich indigo into the brand's signature accent blue (#4054B2), creating a sense of depth and institutional gravitas that immediately communicates trustworthiness. The visual hierarchy should flow like a well-structured business case — each section revealing itself with clarity and purpose, the gradient transitions acting as subtle emotional bridges between content areas. Colorful gradient accents should be used strategically to highlight key service areas and calls to action, injecting warmth and approachability into what could otherwise feel austere, reflecting the firm's promise of partnership "auf Augenhöhe" — at eye level, personal and accessible. The Open Sans typography should feel clean and dependable throughout, anchoring the flowing color work with readable, grounded text.

The design philosophy centers on controlled sophistication through chromatic depth. Typography in Open Sans should carry dual weight — bold, large headlines that feel authoritative and knowledgeable (evoking decades of Mittelstand expertise), paired with lighter body text that reads as approachable and transparent, never intimidating. Interactions and scroll-triggered transitions should feel like smooth, liquid movements — gradients shifting subtly as the user progresses, creating a sense of continuous, unhurried revelation rather than abrupt section breaks. The single-page journey should emotionally progress from an impactful first impression of competence and scale (the hero with its deep navy-to-blue gradient sweep), through a middle section that builds trust through clarity of services and personal commitment, arriving at a final call-to-action that feels warm and inviting — the gradient resolving into lighter, more open tones that say "we're ready when you are." Each service pillar — ÖPNV-Beratung, KMU-Beratung, Controlling, Unternehmensbewertung — should be presented as a distinct yet harmonious element within the gradient narrative, connected by the flowing color language.

Draw inspiration from the feeling of entering a premium yet unpretentious consulting office — the kind with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking a quiet landscape, where the furniture is excellent but not ostentatious, and every detail signals competence without trying too hard. Think of the visual language of contemporary German engineering culture: precision married with understated elegance, the Bauhaus legacy of form following function reinterpreted through a modern lens of layered color and depth. Reference the emotional quality of well-crafted data visualizations where complex information becomes beautiful and immediately comprehensible — that same principle should guide how the consultancy's four service areas are presented. The gradient palette should evoke the feeling of twilight over the Westerwald hills — deep, contemplative blues giving way to clearer, more purposeful tones — creating an atmosphere that is simultaneously established and forward-looking, rooted in regional identity yet operating with metropolitan sophistication.
