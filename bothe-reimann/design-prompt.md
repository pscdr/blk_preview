## Design-Prompt: Bothe-Reimann Tortechnik
**Style: Swiss/International**

Craft a single-page landing page for BOTHE–REIMANN Tortechnik GmbH — a seasoned industrial door and loading technology specialist with over 25 years of experience operating nationwide from the heart of Germany's Westerwald region. Channel the Swiss/International design tradition: a rigorous grid-based layout that communicates precision, reliability, and engineering confidence. The moment a visitor arrives, they should feel the quiet authority of a company that builds things to last — no decoration for its own sake, only purposeful visual order. The color palette anchors on BOTHE–REIMANN's bold signature orange (#FF6622) used sparingly as a commanding accent against generous white space, with their industrial blue (#0097CE) providing systematic counterpoints for interactive elements and secondary calls-to-action. Typography is set in Inter, treated with the disciplined scale and hierarchy that Swiss design demands — large, confident headings that breathe, body text that reads effortlessly. Every section on this single scrolling page should feel like a precisely machined component clicking into place, reflecting the same craftsmanship BOTHE–REIMANN brings to their industrial doors and fire protection systems. Introduce colorful moments through the orange and blue — used not as ornament but as functional signals that guide the eye with the clarity of industrial wayfinding.

The design philosophy is rooted in maximum clarity through minimum means. Typography should feel authoritative yet approachable — the voice of a trusted expert who explains complex industrial solutions in plain language. The Inter typeface, set at generous sizes with careful tracking, creates a reading experience that feels open, modern, and unhurried. Interactions should be precise and immediate — no swooping animations or playful bounces, but crisp state changes and smooth scroll transitions that feel engineered rather than decorated. The single-page journey begins with an arresting hero statement about industrial door expertise, moves through a warm company introduction that establishes their 25-year heritage and nationwide reach, flows into a systematically arranged grid of four core competencies (consultation, quality, professional installation, comprehensive service), and concludes with a clear, confident call to action. Each section transition should feel like turning a page in a perfectly bound technical manual — deliberate, satisfying, complete. The grid is the backbone: visible in the alignment of every element, creating an invisible architecture that makes complex information feel simple and trustworthy.

Think of the atmosphere inside a precision engineering workshop where every tool has its place and every surface is clean — that sense of controlled mastery is the emotional foundation. Draw inspiration from the functional beauty of Bauhaus-era industrial architecture in Germany, where factories were designed with the same care as museums — honest materials, logical proportions, light flooding through steel-framed windows. Channel the feeling of Swiss railway stations: everything runs on time, every sign is legible from distance, every surface communicates competence without saying a word. The orange accent should carry the warmth and energy of molten steel — industrial heat contained within perfect geometric boundaries. The blue should evoke the reliability of structural engineering blueprints — technical, precise, trustworthy. Together on white space, they create a visual language that says: we are modern, we are meticulous, and we have been doing this longer than most. The page should feel like the digital equivalent of a firm handshake from someone who builds things that protect buildings and the people inside them.
