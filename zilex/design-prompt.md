## Design-Prompt: ZILEX
**Style: Neo-Geo (Refined Geometric Patterns, Mathematical Beauty)**

Embrace the Neo-Geo design language to craft a single-page landing page for ZILEX, an AI-powered platform that automates the entire heat exchanger design and ordering process — from fluid specifications to final quote. The core emotional quality should be one of crystalline precision meeting quiet confidence: visitors arriving at this page should immediately sense that they are engaging with a system built on mathematical rigor and engineering excellence. The visual hierarchy flows through interlocking geometric compositions — hexagonal grids, tessellated patterns, and precisely angled intersections — rendered in ZILEX's deep petrol blue (#003854) and vibrant teal accent (#23A08C) against clean white expanses. These geometric motifs should echo the cross-sectional diagrams and tube layouts inherent to heat exchanger engineering, transforming technical blueprints into an aesthetic experience. As users scroll, the mood transitions from bold confidence in the hero section to methodical clarity in the feature breakdowns, arriving at warm trust in the testimonials and call-to-action. Incorporate colorful geometric accents — teal polygons, blue-gradient overlays, subtle pattern fills — to punctuate key sections and guide the eye with purposeful energy. The ZILEX logo (https://zilex.de/images/logo.png) anchors the navigation with understated authority.

The design philosophy channels mathematical beauty into every interaction and typographic choice. Headlines set in Barlow Semi Condensed SemiBold should feel authoritative and engineered — like precisely machined components slotting into place — while body text in Barlow maintains a clean, approachable readability that makes complex thermal engineering concepts feel accessible. The typographic scale follows a deliberate geometric progression, creating rhythm through calculated proportions rather than arbitrary sizing. Interactions should feel snappy and precise, like the click of a precision instrument: hover states that reveal geometric outlines, scroll-triggered sections that tile into view like tessellated surfaces assembling themselves. The single-page journey progresses emotionally from "this is the future of engineering" (awe and ambition in the hero) through "here is exactly why and how" (systematic problem-solution pairs demonstrating speed, simplicity, transparency, and zero errors) to "this is built for you" (targeted audience segments and real customer success stories) and finally "start now" (confident, friction-free call-to-action). Each section transition should feel like turning the facet of a precisely cut gem — a new angle revealing new depth, but all part of one unified crystal structure. The square-cornered buttons in teal (#23A08C) and deep blue (#013B53) reinforce the geometric commitment, rejecting softness in favor of decisive, angular confidence.

Draw inspiration from the feeling of walking through a world-class science museum where every exhibit panel is a masterpiece of information design — where complex systems are rendered with such visual clarity that understanding feels effortless and even beautiful. Channel the aesthetic precision of technical drafting rooms where blueprints carry an inherent elegance, the geometric purity of Islamic tile work where infinite complexity emerges from simple repeating forms, and the confident restraint of Bauhaus-influenced industrial design where every element earns its place through function. Think of the satisfaction found in perfectly aligned engineering tolerances, the visual poetry of computational geometry, and the sophisticated calm of a precision workshop where every tool has its designated position. The abstract quality of crystallographic structures — where atoms arrange themselves into patterns of mathematical inevitability — should inform how content blocks relate to one another: each section positioned with the certainty of molecular bonds, creating a page that feels not designed but discovered, as if this arrangement were the only possible solution. This Neo-Geo approach transforms ZILEX from a software tool into a visual statement about the beauty of engineering itself, where the page's aesthetic precision mirrors the computational precision of the platform it represents.
