## WHY ZILEX?

For decades, sourcing heat exchangers has been a slow and complex process, with fragmented workflows, lack of transparency, and a high risk of errors. These challenges make it difficult for professionals to make informed decisions quickly. Here's what industry professionals struggle with:

### Multiple Process
The procurement of a heat exchanger involves multiple parallel processes such as technical specification, supplier inquiry, commercial evaluation, and internal approvals before the final purchase is made.

### Streamlined Process
Through a unified, digital platform, all stakeholders can access real-time data, while a unique algorithm explores all theoretical possibilities and immediately presents the most suitable individual solutions. With clear processes and dynamic workflows, the procurement process becomes faster and more predictable, significantly reducing bottlenecks.

### Slow & Complex
Due to the need for thermodynamic analysis, strength calculations, and careful material selection, sourcing a heat exchanger becomes a slow and complex process requiring technical precision and multiple layers of approval.

### Speed & Simplicity
By automating the design and procurement process, heat exchanger sourcing becomes faster and more efficient. Streamlined workflows, simplified approvals, and integrated systems eliminate unnecessary delays and reduce complexity, enabling quicker decisions.

### Transparancy Gap
In heat exchanger procurement, transparency is often compromised by unclear design rationales, hidden cost structures, and limited visibility into supplier competencies, complicating the decision-making process.

### Enhanced Transparency
Advanced digital tools and clear documentation provide complete visibility into the design rationale, pricing, and supplier capabilities. This transparency enables professionals to make more informed decisions, ensuring the right solutions at the right cost.

### Risk of Errors
Inaccurate specifications, manual data handling, and fragmented communication increase the risk of errors during the procurement of heat exchangers, potentially leading to costly redesigns or performance issues.

### No Errors
By leveraging automated calculations, customized designs, and integrated quality checks, the risk of errors is eliminated. Real-time validation and precise specifications ensure that every heat exchanger meets all technical requirements without costly mistakes. The solution is guaranteed to be ready to order, providing full confidence in performance and delivery.

## Services We Provide

Easy Access to Technical Design Information

### Quick PED
Module for classifying pressure vessels in accordance with the Pressure Equipment Directive (PED). It guides through essential criteria such as pressure, volume, medium, and temperature to ensure accurate categorization and compliance with European safety standards.

[Start now](https://app.zilex.de/ped/)

### Fluid Properties
Easily find essential thermophysical properties with our Material Property Lookup. Input temperature and/or pressure to access values like density, viscosity, thermal conductivity, specific heat capacity, and vapor pressure.

## Products We Provide

Easy Configuration of Technical Products

### Gaskets
Tool for easily specifying flat gaskets for heat exchangers and pipelines, with price and delivery time details readily available. It simplifies the selection process, facilitating efficient planning and transparent procurement.

[Start now](https://app.zilex.de/gaskets/)

### Tubes
Generating bending plans and tube bundles for straight and U-tubes, including price and delivery time information. It enables precise planning and streamlines procurement through transparent cost and scheduling details.

### Tubesheets
Creating custom tube sheets, including material selection, thickness, and tube layout, along with price and procurement information. It supports tailored designs and facilitates efficient ordering.
