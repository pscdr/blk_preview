# Design-Prompt: Reuth GmbH
**Style: Neo-Geo (Refined Geometric Patterns, Mathematical Beauty)**

Embrace the Neo-Geo design philosophy to craft a single-page website for Reuth GmbH, a German precision toolmaking and injection molding company with over 60 years of mastery. The core emotional quality should evoke the feeling of entering a meticulously engineered space — where every element exists with mathematical purpose and geometric clarity. As visitors arrive, they should sense the convergence of industrial strength and refined order, anchored by Reuth's signature turquoise (#1ABC9C) as the dominant structural color and warm orange (#EF7900) as a deliberate accent that draws attention like a precision tool touching raw material. The visual hierarchy should flow with the certainty of a calibrated machine: bold geometric shapes framing the hero section, interlocking patterns guiding the eye through five core competencies — Werkzeugbau, Silikon-/LSR-Formen, High-Tech Lohnfertigung, Systempartner, and Spritzguss — and resolving into the human element of the team. Typography in Raleway for headings and Dosis for body text should feel like technical lettering elevated to art. Incorporate colorful geometric elements — turquoise polygons, orange accent lines, subtle grid overlays on the light #F6F6F6 background — to create depth and visual rhythm throughout the scroll.

The design philosophy should marry geometric rigor with emotional warmth, reflecting a company where "Tradition meets Innovation." Typography should feel authoritative yet approachable — Raleway's clean geometric letterforms carrying the weight of 60 years of expertise while Dosis adds a modern, friendly cadence to body copy. Interactions and scroll-triggered animations should feel precise and mechanical — elements sliding into position along invisible grid lines, geometric shapes rotating or assembling like components on a production floor, counters ticking upward to reveal 67 years of experience, 50 employees, and 6,134+ molds with the satisfying click of a precision gauge. The single-page journey should progress from awe (the hero showcasing industrial excellence) through confidence (the competency grid demonstrating breadth and depth) to trust (the human faces and direct contact details of Stefan Böhm and his team) and finally to action — the bold orange CTA to call +49 2689 9868-0 feeling like the natural next step in a perfectly constructed sequence. Each section transition should use geometric dividers or angular cuts rather than simple horizontal lines, creating a sense of engineered intentionality.

Draw inspiration from the emotional quality of a master watchmaker's atelier — where every tool has its place and every surface reflects purpose. Channel the feeling of Bauhaus-adjacent industrial spaces in the German Mittelstand: honest materials, visible structure, pride in craft without ostentation. Think of the satisfying geometry found in technical drawings, the tessellated patterns of precision-cut metal, the ordered beauty of a CNC machine's tool rack. Reference the cultural weight of postwar German engineering culture — where reliability became identity and quality became philosophy. The design should carry the quiet confidence of a company that has built over six thousand molds: not flashy, but undeniably excellent. The Neo-Geo patterns should feel like they emerged from the same mindset that produces precision tools — calculated, intentional, beautiful in their exactness. The Reuth logo (https://reuth.de/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Reuth-logo-svg.svg) should sit within this geometric ecosystem as naturally as a keystone in an arch, with the overall composition suggesting that this company doesn't just manufacture — it engineers solutions with the same geometric perfection visible in the design itself.
